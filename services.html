SERVICES

The Biotopo Selva Húmeda Natural Reserve has the basic infrastructure to attend groups of people from public and private entities that demand a peaceful and relaxing place with the logistics to carry out meetings, receptions and events such as: seminars, workshops, meetings, spiritual retreats, etc.

The Biotopo Selva Húmeda Natural Reserve has several water sources available for alternative medicine practices such as Hydrotherapy; Geotherapy and Bio-healing. The accommodations have single and double rooms, restaurant service and a traditional Maloca that serves as a meeting point for connection between man and nature.

Accommodation in eco-lodge equipped with 10 rooms with capacity for 20 people. One room for every two people with a closet and a cabin with cotton linen. Four internal sanitary batteries. Lighting with gas lanterns or candles. Optional accommodation for couples (double bed) in the Maloca (Maloca of the Elves), equipped with 4 double rooms.

  • Restaurant with wood-burning stove and laundry.
  • Eco-Hicking.
  • Birdwatching.
  • Bioenergetic Recharge.
  • Hydrotherapy.
  • Eco-pedagogical lectures.
  • Night walks and social gatherings.
  • Tourist Guide.
