Important facts+ DONATIONS

  • "Biotopo Selva Húmeda" Natural Reserve - 23 years protecting the environment.

    Since its foundation in 1997, the "Biotopo Selva Húmeda" Natural Reserve has been recognized by national and international environmental protection associations.

  • The mammals that live in the Nature Reserve are in danger of extinction due to human impact.

    Illegal trafficking of exotic species has endangered the lives and future of several mammal and bird species over the past ten years.

  • The impact of Glyphosate spraying on the natural habitat of birds.

    "Biotopo Selva Húmeda" Foundation, calls on universities to investigate the impact of glyphosate spraying on the habitat of the hundreds of bird species living in the Nature Reserve.

  • Hundreds of species of birds, insects and amphibians.

    The poisonous frog Epipedobates Narinensis, represents only a small fraction of the hundreds of specimens of flora and fauna recently discovered that live within the territory of the reserve.
