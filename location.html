LOCATION

The Biotopo Selva Húmeda Natural Reserve is located in the south of the Republic of Colombia and in the western part of the Department of Nariño, on the road that leads to the city of Tumaco from the city of Pasto. This road is located in the coastal foothills of the Department of Nariño, 202 km from the city of Pasto and 95 km from the city of Tumaco, on an altitude gradient of 440 to 610 meters above sea level, with a tropical climate of 24 degrees and an area of approximately 2,000 hectares.

Please use the (+) and (-) buttons in the interactive map to see the exact location of the Biotopo Selva Húmeda Natural Reserve.

HOW TO GET THERE

From the city of San Juan de Pasto to the Biotopo Selva Húmeda Natural Reserve, it is possible to travel by the Pan-American Highway to the city of Tumaco in a 4-hour trip to El Diviso, municipality of Berlin, km 95. It is possible to travel by private transportation or different public transportation alternatives. The public transport service is provided by six prestigious inter-municipal transport companies in Nariño.

From the city of San Andrés de Tumaco, it is possible to travel along the Panaericana Highway for two hours.
